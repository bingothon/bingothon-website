Bingothon is going into the next round!

This May, we're holding the second iteration of our event, here are all the dates you need to know:

  • Submissions will last from Sunday, March 24 to Wednesday, April 24.

  • Once the submissions-phase is over, we will work on a schedule and release this as soon as possible.

  • Bingothon will take place on the weekend from May 24 to May 27 on the twitch channel of ESA, make sure to follow it, so you don't miss Bingothon.

  • For more information on submissions we have provided some general guidelines you should follow:

    • The content you submit must be in a "bingo" format. This means no "normal speedruns" are accepted.

    • When submitting a race, all participants have to register their Twitch and Speedrun.com name on our submissions page, however only one person needs to submit. Please list the Twitch names of the other participants in the "Runner nomination" field of the submissions form, after clicking "This is a race / relay / co-op run".

    • Your submission must include a video

    • Please also provide a link to the Speedrun.com leaderboards of your game. In most cases, the form should autofill this.

    • After submitting, please make sure that you joined our Bingothon Discord Server
Once you've read through all these guidelines, you can submit your bingo here.
